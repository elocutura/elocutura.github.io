Chroma flood - Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy Last Updated: 31/05/2026 Halfmoon Games ("we," "our," or "us") operates the color-flood puzzle mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you play our game. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection and Use

Core Game Data Our App is designed to be privacy-friendly. The core game itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data or gameplay metrics on our own servers. Your progress and game states are handled locally on your device. Third-Party Services While the App does not directly collect your data, it integrates trusted third-party services to show advertisements and process in-app purchases. These third parties may collect information sent by your device as part of their services: - Google AdMob (Advertising): We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the game. AdMob may collect and use unique identifiers (such as the Advertising ID on your device) to personalize ads and analyze ad performance. View Google’s Privacy Policy. - RevenueCat (In-App Purchases): We use RevenueCat as a service provider to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions via the Google Play Console. RevenueCat helps us verify your purchase status using anonymous, system-generated identifiers. They do not have access to your credit card or billing details. View RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy. - Google Play Billing (Payment Processing): All financial transactions are handled securely directly by the Google Play Console. We do not collect or store your financial information. View Google Play Services Privacy Policy.

2. Age Restrictions and Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided personal information to the third-party services used in this App, please contact us so we can take steps to ensure it is addressed.

3. Data Security

We care about the security of your information. Because our App relies entirely on trusted, industry-standard third-party SDKs (Google and RevenueCat) for ads and purchases, your data is handled using their secure infrastructure. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
